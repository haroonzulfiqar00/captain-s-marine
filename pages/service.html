Service You Can Count On

The heart of Captain’s Marine is our Certified Service Department—an experienced team dedicated to delivering an elevated ownership experience. Our service advisors guide you through every detail with clarity, while our full-time, year-round technicians work in fully equipped bays to provide fast, precise service. We invest heavily in ongoing training, manufacturer certifications, and MRAA 5 Star standards, and we stock hundreds of replacement parts to keep turnaround times quick. As fellow boaters, we’re committed to getting you back on the water fast and keeping you there for as long as Montana’s summers allow.

ANNUAL WINTER SERVICE

Some shops may offer a “$99 Winterize” that covers the bare-minimum for keeping your boat safe. That’s not how we operate. We know your boat is a meaningful investment, and it deserves proper care. During our Winterize service, our technicians follow the full manufacturer-recommended procedures: fluids, filters, engine alignment, and more, so your boat is protected and performing at its best. When summer returns, you’re ready to get back on the water without missing a beat.

BOAT SUMMERIZATION

When Montana winter gives way to warm weather, our team rolls into a thorough 55-point Summerization Checklist process to make sure your boat is fine-tuned and ready for stress-free days on the lake.

INBOARD SERVICE AND REPAIR

Inboard Service and Repair covers everything from computer diagnostics, tune-ups, and software updates to transmission service, prop-shaft and engine alignment, raw-water pump impeller replacement, and full manufacturer-recommended maintenance - plus any additional repairs your boat may need.

OUTBOARD SERVICE AND REPAIR

Outboard Service and Repair covers everything from computer diagnostics, tune-ups, and software updates to gear-lube changes, inspections for faulty parts, raw-water pump impeller replacement, and full manufacturer-recommended maintenance - plus any additional repairs your outboard may need.

STERNDRIVE SERVICE AND REPAIR

Sterndrive Service and Repair covers everything from computer diagnostics, tune-ups, and software updates to gear-lube changes, drive removal and engine-alignment checks, inspections for faulty parts, raw-water pump impeller replacement, and full manufacturer-recommended maintenance - plus any additional repairs your sterndrive may need.

ACCESSORY INSTALLATION

Our technicians have extensive experience installing a wide range of marine equipment, including downriggers, fish finders, electric bow mounts, thrusters, tables, diving boards, towing accessories, and more - whatever it takes to make your boat the ultimate on-the-water machine, our techs can handle it!
