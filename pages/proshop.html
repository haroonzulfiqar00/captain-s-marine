Most of the Fun Happens Behind The Boat

At the Captain’s Marine Pro Shop, we know that the fun behind your boat is just as important as the boat itself. Our knowledgeable team helps you find the right gear for your style, size, and activity—whether it’s wakesurf boards, skis, tubes, life vests, or apparel. With hands-on expertise, exchanges for the perfect fit, and real-world advice, we make gearing up for the water effortless, fun, and tailored to your family’s adventures.
