About Captain’s Marine

Contact Us

Our Mission

To make your entire boating experience feel effortless, to be your go-to resource for all things boat-related, and to help you create unforgettable memories as fellow boaters.

Our Vision

To be the benchmark for excellence in every aspect of our business by offering highly reputable services, products, and customer experience.

Our Values

We believe in honesty in every aspect of life, treating others with respect, and upholding trust and integrity in all we do. We are committed to continuous improvement and take accountability for ourselves and for one another.

Captain’s Marine operates under a simple belief:

Boat Owners deserve to experience what stress-free boating truly looks like. That belief drives every process, standard, and decision within our business.

What started as a small, energetic shop with a crew of three, has grown into a full-service dealership - well known in the Flathead Valley for its technical expertise, transparency, and customer service that feels personal from the very first conversation.

Our three core principles

To make your entire boating experience feel effortless, to be your go-to resource for all things boat-related, and to help you create unforgettable memories as fellow boaters. We show up with patience, honesty, and a genuine desire to guide, not pressure. That’s the foundation behind every recommendation we make and every service we stand behind.

What truly sets us apart

Our service and parts advisors, sales people, marketing team, technicians, detailers, and transport crew are all dedicated specialists who take pride in problem solving, clear communication, and getting it right the first time. Most of us grew up on these lakes - and we treat every boat like the prized family possession it is.

These are all the wonderful aspects that make Captain’s Marine a great place to do business, and a great place to work - even awarding us the “MRAA Great Dealership to Work For” Award in 2025. But we weren’t always the robust team we are now.
